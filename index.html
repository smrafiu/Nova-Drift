HOW TO PLAY

MOVE

Drag anywhere on screen to fly. Ship follows your finger direction.

AUTO-FIRE

Your ship fires automatically. Focus on dodging and surviving.

GEMS

Collect cyan gems dropped by destroyed enemies for bonus score.

POWER-UPS

Shield, Rapid Fire, Nova Bomb, and Repair spawn during waves.

WAVES

Each wave spawns harder enemies. Boss arrives every 5th wave.

LIVES

You have 3 ships. Losing all ends the mission permanently.
